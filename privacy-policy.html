Privacy Policy

Beacon  ·  Last updated: April 5, 2026

Beacon is a crowdsourced parking safety app for Texas A&M students. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to make the app work:

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Push notification token — a device identifier provided by Apple that allows us to send you parking alerts. You can disable notifications at any time in the app or in your device Settings.
  • Parking lot reports — when you submit a report, we store the lot name, a timestamp, and your account ID so duplicate reports can be managed and the app status can be updated.
  • Photos (optional) — if you choose to attach a photo to a report, that image is uploaded and stored. Photos are never required.

We do not collect your name, phone number, precise location, contacts, browsing history, or any payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate Beacon:

  • Authenticating your account so only verified users can submit reports.
  • Displaying real-time parking lot status to other app users.
  • Sending push notifications when a report is submitted near you.

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics profiles, or any purpose beyond running the app. We do not sell your data to anyone.

3. How Your Information Is Stored

Beacon uses Firebase (a Google service) to store data securely in the cloud. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required to operate the app (Firebase/Google as described above) or as required by law.

Parking lot report data (lot name, time of report) is visible to all signed-in Beacon users — this is the core function of the app. Your email address and account ID are never displayed publicly within the app.

5. Push Notifications

Beacon uses Expo's push notification service to deliver alerts to your device. Your push token is stored in Firebase and shared only with the Expo notification service for the purpose of delivering notifications. You can opt out of notifications at any time inside the app (Account tab → Notifications toggle) or through your iOS Settings.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as you have an account with Beacon. Parking lot reports are retained indefinitely as an anonymized activity log. Photos you upload are retained unless you request deletion.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at austinbsuh@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Beacon is intended for use by college students (age 18 and older). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Beacon after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach out at austinbsuh@gmail.com.
